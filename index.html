Our Legacy

SAIPRASAD BRICKS is a family-run brick manufacturing business located in Georai, Beed, Maharashtra. For years our kiln has produced strong, reliable bricks used in homes, schools, and construction projects across the region. Every brick we fire represents craftsmanship, experience, and commitment to quality.
