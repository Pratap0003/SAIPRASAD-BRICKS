Our Legacy

SAIPRASAD BRICKS is a family-driven brick manufacturing business based in Georai, Beed, Maharashtra. Our kiln has served contractors, farmers, builders and communities for over a decade. From small homes to large projects, our bricks carry the strength of experience and the pride of craftsmanship.
